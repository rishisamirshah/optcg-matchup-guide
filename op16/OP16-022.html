Monkey D. Luffy (OP16-022) (OP16-022)

Green/Blue Impel Down leader with 4 life and 5000 power, built around the [Once Per Turn] leader effect that grants up to 2 DON!! active if all your Characters share the {Impel Down} type. The DON-acceleration plan funds early 5-cost OP16-048 Buggy (6000) and OP16-032 Boa Hancock (7c, 9000) drops to apply multi-attack pressure across a 4-life clock.
OP16-022 Luffy is a behaviorally split deck — going first 47.7% in 256 games and going second 57.0% in 374 games. Across all 790 games it's a 53.9% deck overall, with a -9.3 percentage point play-order swing in favor of going second. The 4-life cap means every contested attack matters, but the DON-active ramp lets Luffy threaten plays a full turn ahead of the printed cost curve. The 5000 leader power means 5000 swings HIT via tie (attacker wins) and 4000 swings FAIL naturally — most opp leaders are 5000 so leader-vs-leader is symmetric. Champion deck (15W-15L over 30 games, 50.0% WR) leans on OP16-032 Boa Hancock (3.60 avg copies, 99.6% of decks), OP16-048 Buggy (3.94 avg, 91.3% of decks), and the OP16-042 event package (6.95 avg copies across 106.9% of decks).
