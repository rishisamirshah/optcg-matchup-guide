Donquixote Rosinante (061) (OP12-061)

Purple/Yellow Trafalgar Law toolbox leader with 4 life and 5000 power. Leader passive 1) lets you replace a [Trafalgar Law] K.O. by adding a life card to hand instead (once per turn); 2) [DON!! 1] reduces the next 4-cost-or-more Law play by 2 cost this turn. The champion build (45W-27L over 72 games, 62.5% WR) crams 4x EB04-038 Rosinante & Law (6c, 8000), 4x EB04-058 Borsalino (5c, 6000), 4x P-088 Trafalgar Law (4c, 5000), 4x P-093 Trafalgar Law (4c, 6000), 4x OP12-108 Rosinante (1c, 2000), 4x I Love You!!, 4x Mamaragan, and 4x OP16-065 Sakazuki (7c, 8000).
Rosinante is a positive-WR leader: 282W-237L over 519 games (54.3% overall). Going first is meaningfully better — 57.4% WR over 270 games vs 53.0% over 132 games going second (+4.4pp swing). The 4-life cap is the tightest in the pool but the Law-protection passive effectively converts one Law K.O. into a life draw, banking a 'fifth life' on a Law-heavy board. The cost-reduction passive accelerates EB03-062 Trafalgar Law (8c, 6000) to a 6-cost play and OP12-073 Trafalgar Law (7c, 8000) to a 5-cost play with one [DON!! 1] activation — the deck snowballs around stacking Law bodies under Rosinante protection. Champion deck (72 games, 62.5% WR) is the best-WR build at the >=50g threshold.

Swing pattern
Yamato leader 5000 HITS Rosinante 5000 via tie. +1 DON to 6000 HITS clearly. Yamato has 1-life buffer over you — the math: Yamato can absorb 5 hits to your 4, so any straight race loses by exactly 1. The Rosinante leader passive (replace Law K.O. with life-card draw) is your effective 5th life, but only if a Law is on board when the K.O. attack lands. Hand disruption can shrink your counter pool, breaking the +2000-to-fail-5000 math.
Mulligan
Keep openings with OP12-108 Rosinante + a 4-cost Law + 2000-counter density (OP12-112 Baby 5 is 2000 counter, OP10-114 X.Drake if splashed). Mulligan slow draws — Yamato's 5-life buffer punishes development hands.
