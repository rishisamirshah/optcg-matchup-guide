Portgas.D.Ace (001) (OP16-001)

Red Whitebeard Pirates aggro leader with 5 life and 5000 power. Leader effect grants Rush to one 8000+ power Whitebeard Pirates or Monkey.D.Luffy character per turn, turning T6-T7 OP16-003 Edward.Newgate (8c, 10000) and OP16-014 Marco (Alternate Art) (6c, 8000) into immediate-impact Speed Attackers. Champion deck floods cheap Whitebeard bodies (OP16-011, OP16-021, OP16-118 Portgas.D.Ace 118 at 6000) and closes with Rush-pumped 10000+ swings.
OP16-001 Ace is behaviorally polarized — 37.7% going first (138g) vs 54.7% going second (159g), a -17.0 percentage-point swing. Across all 370 games it's a 47.3% deck overall. The 5 life gives one more life-buffer than most aggro mirrors and the Rush keyword on 8000+ Whitebeard bodies converts mid-game tempo into immediate damage, which is why going second (where you land bodies on developed DON) is strongly preferred. Champion deck (38g, 55.3% WR) leans on OP16-003 Edward.Newgate (Alternate Art) (100% of decks, 3.68 avg), OP16-015 Monkey.D.Luffy (96.2%, 3.89 avg), OP16-118 Portgas.D.Ace (118) (95.4%, 3.94 avg), OP16-014 Marco (Alternate Art) (93.9%, 3.54 avg), and OP13-016 Monkey.D.Garp (85.5%, 3.99 avg).

What to expect
Teach is a 4-life 5000-power Black/Yellow leader with the Trigger-discard redirect (trash 1 [Trigger] from hand to change an opponent's attack target to leader or a Blackbeard Pirates character). Expect OP09-095 Laffitte (1c, 1000) T1, OP09-011 Hongo (3c, 3000, 2000 counter) T2-T3, EB04-058 Borsalino (5c, 6000) or OP15-114 Wyper (5c, 6000) T4-T5, and OP09-093 Marshall.D.Teach (10c, 12000) T6+ as the finisher.
Swing pattern
Teach leader 5000 HITS Ace 5000 via tie. +1 DON to 6000 HITS clearly. Teach's redirect costs them 1 [Trigger] card per attack dodged — your job is to swing in 3+ times per turn so they can't redirect every shot. OP16-014 Marco (Alternate Art) 8000 + leader-granted Rush is the spine: an 8000 Speed Attacker against a 4-life leader is brutal tempo. Late-game OP09-093 Teach 12000 demands a redirect-of-your-own (cards like OP13-016 Monkey.D.Garp at 2000 counter) or a stacked counter wall.
Mulligan
Keep openings with OP16-014 Marco (Alternate Art) (3.54 avg copies in 93.9% of decks) or OP16-003 Edward.Newgate (Alternate Art) (3.68 avg in 100% of decks). Also valuable: OP16-118 Portgas.D.Ace (118) (3.94 avg in 95.4%) for T5 development. Mulligan hands with no 5-cost+ Whitebeard body — without Rush targets the leader effect can't fire.
