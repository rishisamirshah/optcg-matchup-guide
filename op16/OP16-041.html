Buggy (OP16-041) (OP16-041)

Blue Impel Down swarm leader with 5 life and 5000 power. Leader effect [Once Per Turn]: when an {Impel Down} type Character leaves the field, summon 1 {Impel Down Prisoner} token (1-cost, 1000 power, {Impel Down} type) to your field. The champion build floods cheap Impel Down bodies (OP16-042 at 7.15 avg copies, OP16-055 at 3.93, OP16-056 at 3.99, OP16-054 at 3.94, OP16-045 at 3.84) and closes with OP16-048 Buggy (5c, 6000) plus OP07-056 Slave Arrow board-clears.
Buggy is a 50.8% deck overall (196W-190L over 386 games). Play-order swing is significant: going first 46.9% (69W-78L, 147g) vs going second 56.2% (86W-67L, 153g) — a -9.3 percentage point swing favoring going second. The 5-life buffer matches most opp leaders, but the champion build's 35-game sample sits at 34.3% WR (12W-23L) — the deck swarm-trades but struggles into control. Leader 5000 power means 5000 swings HIT via tie and 4000 swings FAIL naturally. The token spam refunds bodies as opp removes Impel Down characters, generating value via the once-per-turn token summon. Pros lean on OP16-055 (99.4% of decks), OP16-056 (99.4%), OP16-045 (95.8%), OP16-054 (93.3%), and OP16-048 Buggy 6000 (91.5%).
