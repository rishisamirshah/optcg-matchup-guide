Portgas.D.Ace (002) (OP13-002)

Blue/Red Whitebeard Ace deck built around a 3-life 6000-power leader that walls naked 5000 swings, a -2000 leader debuff on the attacker (TCGcsv text says +2000, that's a typo; uniformly -2000 in the sim), and OP13-042 Newgate (SP) as the midgame bomb that draws 2 and attaches 2 DON to your attackers.
Across 235 OP-16-era games Ace is 126W-109L (53.6% WR overall). The play-order split is severe: 37W-47L going first (44.0% WR over 84 games) vs 63W-34L going second (64.9% WR over 97 games) — a -20.9 percentage-point swing favoring going second. Per the tie rule (attacker wins at >=), anything attacking at 5000 FAILS into Ace 6000 naturally; 6000 swings HIT via tie; 7000+ HITS clearly. The leader's -2000 debuff on the attacker shrinks a 7000-10000 threat into something Ace can wall. Champion deck is 22W-12L over 34 games (64.7% WR) leaning on OP13-042 Newgate SP (100.0% of decks, avg 3.94 copies), ST22-015 'I Am Whitebeard!!' (99.1% of decks, avg 3.94), PRB02-008 Marco (98.2% of decks, avg 3.90), OP13-016 Monkey.D.Garp (92.9%, avg 3.96), ST22-002 Izo (90.3%, avg 3.59), OP09-118 Gol.D.Roger (87.6%, avg 1.47), EB04-007 Roronoa Zoro (85.8%, avg 3.18), OP13-043 Otama (85.8%, avg 3.80), OP08-047 Jozu (83.2%, avg 3.54), and OP07-051 Boa Hancock SP (70.8%, avg 2.86).
