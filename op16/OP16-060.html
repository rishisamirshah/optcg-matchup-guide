Sengoku (OP16-060)

Purple Admiral-toolbox leader with 5 life and 5000 power. Leader effect: [Activate: Main] return 8 active DON to your DON deck, then play up to 3 {Admiral} type Character cards with different card names from your hand. Champion build is the OP16-mono shell (Sakazuki OP16-065 8000 / Borsalino OP16-073 8000 + the OP16-063/064/066/067 support package) closing with Mamaragan and OP15-078 Slow-Slow Beam Sword finishers.
Sengoku is a high-DON tempo-swing leader. Across all 331 logged games it is 156W-175L (47.1% WR) with a tiny going-first edge: 47.2% first (161g) vs 45.3% second (95g), play-order swing +1.9 points. The 8-DON Admiral dump is the deck's spine — pay 8 active DON, return them to the DON deck, and field up to 3 different Admiral characters at once (typical line: Sakazuki 8000 + Borsalino 8000 + a third Admiral support body). Champion shell (OP16-060 + Sakazuki 4x + Borsalino 4x + OP16-063/064/065/066/067/073/075/076/077/078 + Mamaragan OP15-078 4x + Inuarashi OP13-061 2x + OP07-076 Slow-Slow Beam Sword 2x) is 11W-10L over 21 games (52.4% WR). The 5-life buffer means Sengoku can race 4-life leaders (Teach, Luffy, Rosinante) but is unfavored vs 5-life leaders that out-control it (Shanks, Enel).
