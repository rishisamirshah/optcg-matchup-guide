2

T1 always plays a 1-cost searcher — never swings leader going 2nd

0 leader attacks on T1-going-2nd across 92 games. DON goes onto Ace as charge, not as a swing.

3

Search target order: Edward.Newgate (SP)OP13-042 first, I Am Whitebeard!!ST22-015 second

Even when not deploying Newgate until T5, he digs for it from T1. The Whitebeard combo (event + Newgate) is the deck's engine.

4

Trash priority for -2k: Hancock → Marco dupes → events. NEVER Jozu/Atmos.

Across 162+ trash activations, Jozu was sacrificed 1×, Atmos 2×. Counter-bodies are sacred.

5

Yamato's DON always attaches to Ace

15 of 15 Yamato attaches went to Ace leader. Ace is the only buff target.
