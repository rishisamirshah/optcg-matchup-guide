🏴‍☠️ OP16 Blackbeard / Teach — game plan

Leader Marshall D. Teach (OP16-080) · Black/Yellow · Life 4 · the #1 OP16 deck (756 ladder matches).
Leader effect: [Opponent's Turn] All of your Characters gain +1 cost.
[On your Opponent's Attack] [Once Per Turn] You may trash 1 card with a [Trigger] from your hand: Change the target of that attack to this Leader or to one of your {Blackbeard Pirates} type Character cards.
↳ In plain terms: (1) every opponent character costs +1 on their turn (a tax that slows their board), and (2) once per opponent turn you may trash a [Trigger] card to REDIRECT an attack onto Teach or a Blackbeard Pirates character — soak it on a body or take it on the 4-life leader instead of a key piece.
⚠ Early-ladder, limited sample. OP16 ranked launched in W21 (~3 days of data); this pools 39 Teach wins across all opponents and skill levels (Elo reset to near-zero for the new mode, so no high-Elo filter is meaningful yet). Treat everything here as directional, not settled. Per-matchup samples (1-3 games each) are too thin to split out — this is the pooled game plan.
