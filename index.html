Ace OP13-002 is one of the few 6000-power leaders in the format. Per the tie rule (attacker wins at >=), anything attacking at 4000 or 5000 FAILS into Ace's 6000 leader naturally; 6000 swings HIT via tie; 7000+ HITS clearly. The leader's -2000 debuff on the attacker (printed text reads +2000 — that's a typo; the sim emits -2000 uniformly across 2,652 observed activations) shrinks a 7000-10000 threat into something Ace can wall. The DON!! x1 passive draws 1 on damage taken or when your 6000+ base character is KO'd (observed 2,473 times). Threat density comes from OP13-042 Newgate SP (12000), PRB02-008 Marco (6000), OP07-051 Hancock SP (8000), OP13-054 Yamato (6000), EB04-007 Zoro (9000), and OP09-118 Roger (13000) as a finisher.
